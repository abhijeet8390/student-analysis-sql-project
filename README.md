# Student Analysis SQL Project

## Overview
Analyzed a dataset of 5000 students using MySQL to uncover insights 
about academic performance, attendance patterns, and factors affecting grades.

## Database Structure
4 tables:
- **students** - Student demographics and department info
- **grades** - Exam scores, assignments and final grades
- **attendance_study** - Attendance %, study hours, sleep hours
- **family_background** - Parent education, income level, stress level

## Business Questions Answered
1. What is the gender distribution of students?
2. Which department has the highest average score?
3. Do students with high attendance get better grades?
4. Does family income affect academic performance?
5. Which department has the most failures?
6. How does stress level impact grades?

## Key Findings
- Only 16 out of 5000 students scored Grade A
- Mathematics has the most failing students (76)
- High attendance does NOT guarantee good grades
- Family income has NO significant impact on grades
- Students who fail study MORE hours on average

## Tools Used
- MySQL 8.0
- MySQL Workbench
